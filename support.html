FAQ and Support

Am I able to order custom paint jobs on my old skateboard?

Yes. Urban Culture loves to give older boards new life with one of our custom paint jobs. Just go into the shopping cart feature, select "Customer's own board" and choose which design you would like. You will receive an email regarding delivery information and instructions.

How long is Delivery?

Urban Culture is an Australian owned company located in Queensland. Shipping withing Australia should take between 1-6 business days. However, International shipping will take anywhere from 14 to 30 business days. After your purchase, you will receive an email with your tracking number and estimated delivery time. Please take note, delivery times are at the descresion of the courier or postal service and Urban Culture takes no responsibility for late delivery.

What if my goods are damaged?

If, upon delivery, you find your goods have been damaged or you are not happy with the quality of the product then please contact us via email or phone. We will review your case and provide details on returning your board for refundment or replacement.

Can I have a free board?

Maybe...Urban Culture holds regular custom paint design competitions. You can submit as many designs as you like and enter as many competitions as you like. If your design wins then it will be put into production and be made Limited Edition which you will receive 1x free. All details for our competitions will be displayed on our facebook page so be sure to like our page so you don't miss out.

How do I become involved in the Urban Culture community?

The best way to go about getting involved is to like us on social media (facebook, Instagram, Twitter). We will be posting consistent information regarding competitions, gatherings and news on these pages. Urban Culture is a community and we encourage everyone to get involved.
