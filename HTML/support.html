    FAQ and Support

    Can Urban Culture Do a Custom Paint On My Own Board?

    Yes. Urban Culture loves to give older boards new life with one of our custom paint jobs. Just go into the shopping cart feature, select "Customer's own board" and choose which design you would like. You will receive an email regarding delivery address/instructions for your board.

    How long is delivery?

    Urban Culture is an Australian owned company located in Queensland. Shipping withing Australia should take between 1-6 business days. However, International shipping will take nywhere from __ to __ days. After your purchase, you will receive an email with your tracking number and estimated delivery time. Please take note, delivery times are at the descresion of the delivery companies and we are not responsible for late delivery.

    What if my goods are damaged?

    If, upon delivery, you find your goods have been severly damaged or not of Urban Culture quality then please contact Urban Culture and we will review your case and provide details on returning your board for either a refund or a replacement board.

    Can I have a free board?

    Maybe... Urban Culture hold regular custom board paint design comititions. You can submit as many designs as you like and enter as many compititions as you like. If your design wins then it will be put into production and be made into a limited edition board which you will receive one of for free. All details for our competitions will be displayed on our facebook so be sure to like our page so you don't miss out.

    How can I get more involved in the Urban Culture comunity?

    The best way to go about getting involved is to like us on social midea (facebook, Instagram, twitter). We will be posting all information regarding compintitions, gatherings, skating and technology news on these pages. Urban Culture is a community and we encorage everyone to get envolved.
