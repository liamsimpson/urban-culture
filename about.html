ABOUT US

We at UrbanCulture pride ourselves on providing both a product but also the style and flair that our products embody. We aim to provide electric motorised, unique and affordable boards with style. Our boards embody who we are as a company and give off a certain personal flair. We’re all individuals and are all unique so why not show it? We offer a range of boards with unique and quirky patterns our artists design which are available for purchase or why not specify you own design? With our unique clear grip tape we can paint a design into the deck of your board, seal it and with the clear grip tape you can see your amazingly painted design underneath, contact us with a design for more details. Alternatively, why not send as an image and we’ll print it on high quality vinyl wraps that are normally used to wrap cars and buildings it’s super tough and we’ll seal it and cover it with our unique clear grip tape.


We are the only company in the market providing such a unique service and you won’t find electric boards with this much customisation anywhere else. See those people on their hoverboards and other electric boards that all look the same, don’t be “one of those people” contact us today with your design and we can see about making a board as unique as you are. We are a unique player in this market and are based primarily online and don’t have any physical store fronts, this helps us keep our costs low and pass the low costs onto our customers.
